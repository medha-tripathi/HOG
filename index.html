The term “college experience” has always been vague to me, but I am sure of 2 things: it’s some sort of a journey and it happens when you are in college. Being a covid era student, all of my experiences are divided into online and offline modes. People might try to motivate you by telling that you need to study only through your school years and chill during the college life, but that isn’t true. You'll know what I'm talking about, just bear with me for some for time. Before that have a look at my beautiful beautiful college.




ONLINE:

Waking up with a meet link ping and ending the day by clicking on the "leave meet" button was not at all an awesome experience, atleast not for me. But not everything was flawed, we had our fun that way also. Waking up 5 minutes before the class, having our unofficial google meets, Binging movies and series, and having a close to hostel experience when we all use to listen songs on Discord etc etc.

OFFLINE:

So finally all of our wishes came true and we're called at our beloved campus for our offline classes. Nervousness and excitement hitting at the same time. And meeting everyone was literally worth the wait.
Everyone literally fell in love with ranchi's beautiful weather.


We did a lot of planned and unplanned things together. Did a lot of fun with our seniors, batchmates and faculty as well. Though our hostel is not our actual home but with these fellows by my side, it felt like our second home here. Right now we're at our interim campus in Dhurwa and our fellow boys are at JUT campus in Namkun. Our permanent campus(in KANKE) is under construction and would be ready till 2023 and we would be the first batch to shift to our permanent campus(fingers crossed). I'm learning a lot of things here. Meeting different people with different abilities and talents is very inspiring to me. Our studies and college is running very smoothly. Besides that we're also enjoying our life here(wink) cuz after all college is all about exploring, learning, making mistakes and then learning from them. Playing sports, learning about different clubs and societies, enjoying mess food(not really:\) and growing is fun. I'm personally most excited about volleyball, badminton, chess and all the technical stuff. Being guided by our respected seniors is of great help.



Our Unofficial Trips;)

While waiting for our seniors and college authorities to plan an official trip for us, we didn't sit idle actually. And with our fellow batchmates in another campus (20 km far), we had to plan our little unofficial trips to different places in our beautiful Ranchi city. This city is full of scenic beauties like waterfalls, gardens, dams and the list goes on. So in our journey of exploring different things, we all did explored the city as well. Colleging!!

And now it's time for real talks. We had our share of fun here, no complaints about that whatsoever. 2 hard slaps to those wo say college life is all about attending concerts, bunking classes, having a shahrukh khan entry etc etc and also 2 harder slaps to those who say college is all about studying day and night. Actually, it's all about expanding socially and mentally. Not to sugar coat everything, we are facing our share of problems too. But in the process we're paving a beautiful way for our future batches. This institute is now an inseperable part of me, the mess, the hostel, the classrooms even every single person working here is like a family.
